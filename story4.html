Book 3:

After the defeat of Humbaba, the goddess Ishtar proposes to Gilgamesh. But he rejects and mocks her. When she tells the other gods of his arrogant behavior, they release the Bull of Heaven to punish Gilgamesh. Enkidu and Gilgamesh, however, kill the bull. While they’re celebrating, Ishtar appears and they mock her again. The gods then curse Enkidu with a mysterious illness. After several weeks, he dies, and Gilgamesh and the whole city of Uruk grieve for the great hero.
